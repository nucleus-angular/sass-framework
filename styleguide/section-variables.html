Variables

There are a number of pre-defined global variables that you can use.

Auto Complete

These are the variable the control the look and feel of the auto complete.

  • $border-options-ac 1px solid shade($color-gray, $percentage-darkest)
  • $background-color-options-ac  
  • $max-height-options-ac 300px
  • $background-color-option-is-focused-ac $color-link-water
